StampExplore

Figma - Wireframing & Prototyping

StampExplore is a souvenir collection for users to collect virtual stamps to document their trips. Add a city, and a unique stamp will be added to your collection. Each stamp is linked to an album where users can upload pictures and descriptions from their trip.

Wardrobe Visualizer

Figma - Component Properties

As someone who prefers to online shop but wants to try on the clothes to see what it would look like, the Wardrobe Visualizer helps me "try on" the clothes, and mix & match it with other articles of clothing either from my closet, or other online products. Uses the boolean aspect of component properties.

Flappy Bird: Space

Figma - Interactive Components

A space-themed variation of FlappyBird, the goal is to make it to the end by dodging obstacles, and avoiding the ground. Made with interactive components to control the movement of FlappyBird, obstacles, and backdrops. Avoid hitting the obstacles and/or the ground to win!
